INSTRUCTIONS

PIG is a dice game with the objective of reaching 100p with as few rounds as possible!

Press the "Roll Dice" button to gather points.
Press the "Hold" button to end the round & add your current points to your total points.
If you roll a 1 your turn will end and you'll lose all your current points for the round.

If you understood the rules please enter your name and then let's play!

Objective: Reach 100 points in as few rounds as possible!

Be careful! If you roll a one, you'll lose all your current points for the round!
